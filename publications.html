Recent Publications

Peer-reviewed research and working papers from our team's work across development sectors in South Asia.

Journal Article

Women's Collective Action and Financial Inclusion: Evidence from Self-Help Groups in West Bengal

Authors: Sharma, R., Patel, A. Journal: Development Policy Review Year: 2024

Using original survey data from 2,400 women across rural West Bengal, we examine how participation in government-supported self-help groups affects financial inclusion outcomes. Mixed-methods analysis reveals that collective decision-making processes mediate individual empowerment effects.

Financial Inclusion Women's Empowerment Mixed Methods India

Working Paper

Climate Migration and Urban Service Access: Participatory Research from Bangladesh

Authors: Hassan, T., Fernando, N. Series: PinPoint Working Papers Year: 2024

This study employs photovoice methodology with climate migrants in Chittagong's informal settlements to understand barriers to urban service access. Community-led research reveals informal governance networks that both enable and constrain access.

Climate Migration Urban Services Photovoice Bangladesh

Book Chapter

Participatory Evaluation in Conflict-Affected Areas: Lessons from Northeast India

Authors: Patel, A., Kumari, S. Book: Community-Led Research in South Asia Year: 2024

Drawing on fieldwork in Manipur, we analyze how community-based participatory research can address ethical challenges of conducting research with conflict-affected populations. The chapter provides a framework for trauma-informed and culturally responsive evaluation approaches.

Participatory Research Conflict Settings Ethics Northeast India

Impact Evaluation

Digital Financial Services for Garment Workers: RCT Evidence from Dhaka

Authors: Hassan, T., Khanna, A. Client: International Labour Organization Year: 2023

Randomized controlled trial with 3,000 garment workers evaluating mobile money training programs. Results show significant increases in savings behavior and remittance efficiency, with particularly strong effects for women workers with primary education or higher.

Digital Finance Labor Migration RCT Bangladesh
Executive Summary Technical Report
Policy Analysis

Social Protection and Digital Identity: Aadhaar Implementation Across Indian States

Authors: Sharma, R., Patel, A., Kumari, S. Journal: Economic & Political Weekly Year: 2023

Comparative analysis of Aadhaar implementation in social protection programs across five Indian states. Using administrative data and qualitative interviews, we examine how digital identity systems affect inclusion and exclusion in welfare programs.

Social Protection Digital Identity Policy Analysis India
Full Article Data Visualization
Methodology

Adapting Survey Methods for Low-Literacy Populations: Field Experiences from South Asia

Authors: Khanna, A., Fernando, N. Journal: Survey Research Methods Year: 2023

This methodological paper presents innovations in survey design for populations with limited literacy, drawing on field experiences across India, Bangladesh, and Sri Lanka. We evaluate audio-based questioning, visual response scales, and mobile data collection adaptations.

Survey Methods Literacy Technology South Asia

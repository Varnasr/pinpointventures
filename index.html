About Us

PinPoint Ventures is a specialist research and evaluation agency dedicated to the development and social impact sectors. We deliver insights that help mission-driven organizations, foundations, and multilaterals design, implement, and improve programs across India and South Asia. Our work is rooted in methodological rigor, human-centered research, and a commitment to building evidence for action.

Our Services

  • Program Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL)
  • Baseline and Endline Studies
  • Field Surveys and Mixed-Methods Research
  • Knowledge Product Development
  • Impact Evaluation and Strategy Advisory

About Us

PinPoint Ventures is a specialist research and evaluation partner for organizations committed to social good. We work at the intersection of data, human-centered inquiry, and systems thinking to help programs maximize their effectiveness, equity, and impact. Founded by senior researchers with extensive field experience, we bring methodological rigor and practical insight to every engagement — from remote field sites to policy advisory tables.

Our Approach

  • Rigor: Using sound, context-tailored methodologies.
  • Empathy: Centering community voices in research.
  • Partnership: Co-creating learning with clients.
  • Integrity: Prioritizing transparency and ethics.

Our Services

  • Program Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL)
  • Baseline, Midline, and Endline Surveys
  • Qualitative and Ethnographic Research
  • Knowledge Product Development
  • Impact Assessment and Data Analysis
  • Theory of Change and Strategic Advisory
  • Systems Mapping for Complex Interventions

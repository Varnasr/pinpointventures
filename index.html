Recent Research Examples

Examples from our recent work demonstrating our approach to generating policy-relevant evidence

🏛️ Women's Self-Help Groups Study

Mixed-methods research examining how participation in government-supported SHGs affects women's financial autonomy, household decision-making, and community leadership across rural areas in West Bengal.

"How do different models of SHG support affect women's economic empowerment, and what role do social norms play in mediating these effects?"

🌊 Climate Migration Research

Participatory research with climate migrants in Chittagong's informal settlements, using photovoice and community mapping to understand barriers to healthcare, education, and social protection services.

"What institutional barriers prevent climate migrants from accessing urban services, and how do community networks fill these gaps?"

📱 Digital Financial Services Impact

Randomized controlled trial evaluating mobile money training for garment workers in Dhaka, with focus on remittance patterns, savings behavior, and household financial management.

"Does training in digital financial services increase savings rates and remittance efficiency among internal migrants?"

Feature Wishlist

Research innovations we're developing to better serve communities

Interactive dashboards that allow communities to track program progress and provide feedback directly to implementers and policymakers.

AI-powered tools to analyze qualitative interviews in local languages, preserving nuance while enabling faster pattern recognition across large datasets.

System to track how research findings flow into policy documents, program changes, and ultimately affect community outcomes over time.

Automated systems that ensure research findings are shared back to participating communities in accessible formats and local languages.

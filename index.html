About Us

PinPoint Ventures is a specialist research and evaluation agency dedicated to the development and social impact sectors. We deliver evidence-driven insights that power better programs, stronger strategies, and meaningful change. From baseline studies to complex evaluations, we work across India and South Asia to support mission-driven organizations.

Our Services

  • Program Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL)
  • Baseline and Endline Studies
  • Field Research and Survey Design
  • Knowledge Product Development
  • Impact Assessments and Data Analysis

Impact Areas

We work across a range of sectors, including public health, education, climate resilience, gender equality, livelihoods, and social protection. Our research is designed to inform actionable, lasting solutions.

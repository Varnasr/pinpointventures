The People Behind PinPoint

A diverse team of researchers, economists, and field practitioners united by our commitment to community-centered research and equitable development across South Asia.

Our Journey

PinPoint Ventures emerged from years of field experience across South Asia, where we witnessed both the power of rigorous research and the limitations of extractive research practices that failed to serve communities.

Founded by development practitioners who had worked in government, academia, and civil society, we established PinPoint to bridge the gap between methodological rigor and community-centered approaches.

Today, we combine deep technical expertise with extensive networks across India, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, and Pakistan, enabling us to conduct research that is both academically sound and contextually grounded.

Our Values

These principles guide how we approach research, build relationships, and measure success in our work.

🤝

Partnership

We work with communities as partners, not subjects, ensuring research serves local priorities and builds long-term relationships based on mutual respect and shared learning.

🔍

Rigor

We maintain the highest standards of methodological quality while adapting approaches to fit context, ensuring our research can inform policy and withstand academic scrutiny.

⚖️

Equity

We center marginalized voices and examine how development interventions affect different groups, with particular attention to power dynamics and structural inequalities.

🌱

Impact

We measure success by whether our research leads to better policies, stronger programs, and improved outcomes for the communities we work with across South Asia.
